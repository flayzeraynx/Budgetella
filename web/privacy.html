1. General

This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is processed through the Budgetella mobile application ("App") and the budgetella.app website. We act as "data controller" under applicable privacy law.

In one sentence: We never touch your bank, we never sell your data, we never share it with third-party marketers.

2. Data we collect

2.1 Account information

  • Email address (required)
  • Name / display name (optional)
  • Password — stored as a one-way bcrypt hash, never readable

2.2 Financial data (only what you enter)

  • Transactions you add manually: amount, category, date, note
  • Data extracted from receipt scans (OCR output)
  • Transaction text from voice entry
  • Your budget and goal settings

We never collect bank account details, credit card numbers, or any open-banking data.

2.3 Device & usage

  • Device model, OS version, app version
  • Anonymous session duration, crash logs
  • IP address (security only, anonymized after 30 days)

3. How we use your data

We process your data only for:

  • Service delivery: managing your account, storing transactions, calculating budgets.
  • Personalized insights (Budgi): our AI assistant analyzes your data to provide spending summaries and suggestions.
  • Security: detecting unauthorized access attempts.
  • Communication: important account updates (mandatory), optional product news (if subscribed to newsletter).
  • Legal obligations: responding to official court or regulatory requests.

We never: sell your data to ad networks, share it with third-party marketers, or use it for audience targeting.

4. Sharing

We share your data in limited cases with the following processors:

  • Hosting & infrastructure: Firebase (Google Cloud) — encrypted data storage and authentication.
  • Google Gemini API (AI provider): When you use Budgi or receipt scanning, relevant data (anonymized spending summary or receipt photo) is sent to Google's Gemini API over an encrypted channel. This data is not used for model training and is not retained beyond 30 days. Data transfer only occurs after explicit consent.
  • Crash reporting: Error logs with personal data filtered out.

All providers are bound by a Data Processing Agreement (DPA).

5. Storage & security

  • Location: Data is stored on Firebase infrastructure.
  • Encryption: AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit.
  • Retention: If you delete your account, all your data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

6. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to:

  • Know whether your personal data is being processed
  • Access a copy of your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability (export as JSON via Settings)
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email info@budgetella.app. We respond within 30 days at no charge.

For users in Turkey: your rights are governed by Law No. 6698 (KVKK). All rights listed above apply under Article 11 of KVKK.

7. AI features & third-party data sharing

Budgetella's AI features (receipt scanning, voice entry categorization, Budgi insights) use the Google Gemini API. Only the minimum necessary data is sent, anonymized where possible, and never used for training. You can disable AI features in Settings.

8. Cookies & tracking

The marketing website (budgetella.app) uses Google Analytics (GA4) for anonymous traffic analysis — page views, session duration, referral sources. No cross-site tracking, no advertising cookies. The mobile app does not use cookies.

9. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we'll notify you via in-app notification and/or email at least 14 days in advance. Continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

Questions or requests: info@budgetella.app
We aim to respond within 2 business days.
