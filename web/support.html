Getting started

Download from the App Store, create a free account with your email or Apple/Google Sign-In, and add your first transaction. The 7-day free Premium trial starts automatically — no card required.
Tap the + button at the bottom of the screen. You can scan a receipt (AI reads it automatically), use voice entry ("coffee 4 dollars"), or enter it manually.
Go to Settings → Budgets. Set a monthly spending limit per category (e.g. $500 for Groceries). You'll get a notification when you're getting close to the limit.

Features

Any printed receipt with a visible total amount. Works with supermarket, restaurant, and utility receipts. Photographed receipts and screenshots also work.
Tap the mic icon and say something like "coffee 4.50" or "groceries 85 dollars". AI extracts the amount and suggests a category. You confirm and it's logged instantly.
Budgi is Budgetella's AI assistant. It analyzes your spending patterns and offers weekly insights — biggest expense, category trends, spending pace. It uses Google Gemini API and never stores your data beyond 30 days.

Account & Billing

App Store → Account → Subscriptions → Budgetella → Cancel. Cancel at least 24 hours before renewal. You keep Premium access until the current period ends.
Settings → Account → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're logged in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
Settings → Account → Delete Account. All your data is permanently deleted within 30 days. This action is irreversible — export your data first if needed.
